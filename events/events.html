Showcase of Stars

Event: Annual Showcase of Stars

Each spring we take time to honor the talents and achievements of Mission Hill youth, youth workers, and community members at the Showcase of the Stars. Last June over 200 people attended to celebrate the fantastic accomplishments of youth in Mission Hill. Throughout the evening youth highlight their talents through song, dance, martial arts and spoken word.

Date: June 2nd 2017

Location: Boston MA

More info email: missionhillyouthcollaborative@gmail.com

Halloween on The Hill

Event: Halloween on The Hill

Every Halloween, over 200 children and youth participate in safe, fun and spooky Halloween activities across Mission Hill. There is a haunted house, pony rides, trick or treating, healthy snacks, games and prizes.

Date: October 31st

Location: Boston MA
